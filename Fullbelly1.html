Who We Are

FullBelly is a mission-driven platform dedicated to reducing food waste and hunger by connecting food donors with NGOs in real-time. We believe that no edible food should go to waste while people go hungry.

Reduce Waste

Preventing perfectly good food from ending up in landfills

Empower Communities

Supporting local NGOs and community initiatives

Feed the Hungry

Ensuring nutritious meals reach those who need them most

Sustainable Future

Reducing carbon footprint through food waste prevention
